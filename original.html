Martini

Ingredients

  • 50ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin Original
  • 10ml dry vermouth
  • 2 dashes of aromatic bitters
  • Fresh lemon zest

Instructions

  • Add gin and vermouth to a mixing glass filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a martini glass
  • Add the Grapefuit bitters and finish with lemon zest

Dimpsey Sour

Ingredients

  • 50ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin Original
  • 25ml fresh lemon juice
  • 15ml simple syrup
  • 6 segments of mandarin
  • 1 egg white / vegan alternative

Instructions

  • Muddle the mandarin segments in a shaker to release the juice
  • Add the other ingredients and first shake without using ice for 30 seconds
  • Then put some ice into the shaker and shake again for another 15 seconds (This shaking method will give you some silky foam)
  • Fine strain into a glass of your choice over ice and garnish with some mandarin segments
