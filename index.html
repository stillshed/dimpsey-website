Dimpsey Martini

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 15ml dry vermouth
  • 2 dashes of Grapefruit bitters
  • Fresh lemon zest

Instructions

  • Add gin and vermouth to a mixing glass filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a martini glass
  • Add the Grapefuit bitters and finish with lemon zest

Gin & Juice

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 30ml grapefruit juice
  • Sliced grapefruit

Instructions

  • Combine gin and grapefruit juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a chilled martini glass
  • Garnish with grapefruit slice

Where it's made

Dimpsey Somerset Gin is lovingly hand-crafted in our converted barn, Still Shed Distillery.

Our distillery's location is as rural as the taste of our Somerset Gin, nestled amongst brambles and fields, Dimpsey Somerset Gin is distilled in our copper pot stills, using only the finest, locally sourced botanicals - most of which we grow ourselves!
