Using traditional copper pot stills, just before Dimpsey (that’s old Somerset for sunset), at the foot of the Mendip Hills,
using locally foraged botanicals and Cheddar spring water, this is
Dimpsey Somerset Gin.

Nose

Fragrant juniper, grapefruit & cinnamon

Palette

Smooth & Full-bodied, balanced with grapefruit and mandarin peel freshness

Finish

A long extra dry finish, smooth enough to enjoy on the rocks!

Best enjoyed as a gin and tonic. Fill a glass with lots of ice, top with a premium tonic and a curl of pink grapefruit or mandarin peel to garnish.

Cucumber Cooler

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 22.5ml fresh lime juice
  • 15ml sugar syrup
  • 15ml soda water
  • 2 slices of peeled cucumber
  • 8 mint leaves

Instructions

  • Muddle cucumber in the base of a shaker
  • Add gin, lime juice, sugar syrup and mint
  • Shake with ice and fine strain into chilled glass
  • Top with splash of soda

Gin & Juice

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 30ml grapefruit juice
  • Sliced grapefruit

Instructions

  • Combine gin and grapefruit juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a chilled martini glass
  • Garnish with grapefruit slice
