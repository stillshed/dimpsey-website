A fresh finish, smooth enough to enjoy on the rocks!

Our award winning original dry gin is infused with fresh strawberries and raspberries for 2 days. Fill a glass with lots of ice, top with a premium tonic or sparkling lemonade and a few fresh strawberries or raspberries to garnish.

Please note: the spirit takes the natural colouring from the fruit and therefore will eventually fade. To maintain the colour, please store in a dark place, away from sunlight.

Hand crafted in very small batches of 60 bottles, filled, labelled and numbered by hand.
