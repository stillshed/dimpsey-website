DIMPSEY

SOMERSET GIN

Using traditional copper pot stills, just before Dimpsey (that’s old Somerset for sunset), at the foot of the Mendip Hills,
using locally sourced botanicals and Cheddar spring water, this is
Dimpsey Somerset Gin.

Nose

Fragrant mandarin, grapefruit & cinnamon

Palette

Smooth & Full-bodied, balanced with grapefruit and mandarin peel freshness

Finish

A long extra dry finish, smooth enough to enjoy on the rocks!

Best enjoyed as a gin and tonic. Fill a glass with lots of ice, top with a premium tonic and a curl of pink grapefruit or mandarin peel to garnish.

Dimpsey Martini

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 15ml dry vermouth
  • 2 dashes of aromatic bitters
  • Fresh lemon zest

Instructions

  • Add gin and vermouth to a mixing glass filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a martini glass
  • Add the Grapefuit bitters and finish with lemon zest

Mulled Dimpsey

Ingredients

  • 100ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 400ml apple juice
  • 1 bay leaf, 4 cloves, 2 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick, 1/2 tsp corriander seeds and 1 tsp runny honey

Instructions

  • Divide the Dimpsey between four small heatproof glasses
  • Tip the apple juice into a saucepan with the rest of the ingedients. Heat gently until simmering, then strain in to a jug
  • Pour the mulled apple juice into the glasses with the gin and stir gently to combine

Where it's made

Dimpsey Somerset Gin is lovingly hand-crafted in our converted stable, Still Shed Distillery.

Our distillery's location is as rural as the taste of our Somerset Gin, nestled amongst brambles and fields, Dimpsey Somerset Gin is distilled in our copper pot stills, using only the finest, locally sourced botanicals - most of which we grow ourselves!
